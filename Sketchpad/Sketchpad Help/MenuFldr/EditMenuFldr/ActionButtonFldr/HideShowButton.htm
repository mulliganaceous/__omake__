Hide/Show Button

This command is available only when at least one object is selected.

It creates a button that hides or shows each selected object. Normally the button’s label changes from Hide to Show depending on whether the objects it controls are visible or not. (When some objects are visible and some are not, the button is labeled Hide.)

You can use the Hide/Show panel of Properties to change various aspects of the button’s behavior. For example, you can make the button always show its objects or always hide its objects, instead of toggling between hiding and showing. You can also use this panel to prevent the button from selecting its objects after showing them or to make it show or hide its objects instantly instead of fading them in or out.
