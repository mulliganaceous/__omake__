Measuring Circles and Arcs

You can measure:

·         the Circumference of a circle.

·         the Radius of a circle or an arc.

·         the Area of a circle.

·         the Equation of a circle.

·         the Arc Length of an arc.

·         the Arc Angle of an arc.
