Creating Captions

When you point the Text tool at an empty place in the sketch, the tool appears as an open hand.

· Press and drag to create and begin typing and editing a new caption. Dragging determines the width of your new caption.

To finish editing your caption, press the Esc key or click anywhere outside the caption.
